✓ Adequate renal function — standard DOAC doses apply.

📋 Monitoring & Follow-up

  • Confirm diagnosis with imaging if not already done
  • Assess for underlying provoked vs unprovoked PE
  • Consider duration of anticoagulation (minimum 3 months for provoked, extended for unprovoked)
  • Reassess bleeding risk before prescribing anticoagulation
  • Refer to Thrombosis Canada PE: Diagnosis & Treatment Clinical Guide

❤️ Atrial Fibrillation — Acute Management

Anticoagulation guidance for AF — CCS 2020 Guidelines

1Patient Stability

⚠ Urgent Intervention Required

  • Consider urgent electrical cardioversion at 150–200 J if rate control not effective
  • Antithrombotic therapy: Initiate immediate OAC in ED and continue for ≥ 4 weeks if any "high-risk" features present
  • Early follow-up to review long-term OAC
